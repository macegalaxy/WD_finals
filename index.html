About Jollibee

Since 1978, Jollibee has been bringing joy to families and friends with our delicious food and warm service. What started as an ice cream parlor has grown into the Philippines' largest fast food chain, known worldwide for our signature Chickenjoy and sweet-style spaghetti.

Our mission is simple: to serve great-tasting food that brings joy to everyone. We're not just about the food – we're about creating moments of happiness and togetherness.
